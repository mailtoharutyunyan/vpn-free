1. Commitment to User Privacy and No-Log Policy

VPN-FREE is built on a strict no-log policy. We do not collect, store, or log user activity, browsing history, IP addresses, DNS queries, or connection logs that can be tied back to any individual user.

We minimize data collection while ensuring essential functionality:

  • Payment Data: Email, subscription plan, and payment method via secure third-party processors.
  • Support Data: Messages and emails you send for support purposes.

These third parties do not have access to user activity data related to VPN usage.

5. Data Sharing and Transfers

We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

6. Data Security Measures

We protect your data with industry-standard security including encryption, access controls, and monitoring. We do not retain any logs of VPN usage that could identify you.

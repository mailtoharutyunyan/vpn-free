VPN-FREE is built on a strict no-log policy. We do not collect, store, or log user activity, browsing history, IP addresses, DNS queries, or connection logs that can be tied back to any individual user.

We only collect minimal information required to:

Provide and maintain the Services,

Manage accounts and subscriptions,

Respond to support inquiries,

Monitor and improve app performance,

Ensure security and compliance.

All collected data is anonymized or pseudonymized wherever applicable and never associated with VPN usage activity.
